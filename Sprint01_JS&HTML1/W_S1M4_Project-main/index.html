Overview

WLOP, real name Wang Ling (Chinese 王凌), is a Chinese artist who makes digital art for free on the internet. Born June 5, 1987, he is known for his works in the fantasy genre, and tends to feature women as the focal point of his digital paintings. He also has made a lot of painting-process videos, where he'll show his process during a time lapsed video set to related music.

He's a pretty private person, so not much is known of the artist behind the art, but his works tend to speak for themselves. He's known for a dualistic style, where the foreground elements tend to be crisp and clear, while the backgrounds can be almost watercolor-esque and blurry. However, he still manages to imply a lot of detail where there is none.

Works

WLOP's works include illustrations, comics, and more recently he's even released some animated wallpapers. His earliest posts the author of this site could find date back to Febuary of 2012, and he has since posted hundreds of pieces of art. Most of his work is of his own original characters, but he has made some fanart as well. Almost all of his original works are set in one of three worlds he's crafted, and tend to follow a theme.

Illustrations

As mentioned previously, WLOP tends to base his original art within one of three worlds; the world of Ghost Blade, of Cloud Insect, and of Dead Dreams. Ghost Blade is set in a fantasy world, and features royals, knights, elves, and many more fantastical creatures. Cloud Insect is set in a sci-fi world, often showing technological city-scapes and cyborgs. Dead Dreams seems to be a clash of magic and modern, where mythical creatures can be seen in amongst modern architecture.

A fantasy-themed artwork, set in the Ghost Blade universe. Depicting a dragon and a woman in a frozen scene.

Comics

There are two comics that have been released by WLOP; the first is titled Ghost Blade (鬼刀), and the second is named Cloud Insect (雲蟲). Ghost Blade has been released both with the original Chinese, and a translation in English, and is still being released to this day. Cloud Insect has had a couple chapters released in Chinese, but no official English translation has been made. It has also been put on hiatus while the artist focuses on his first comic. There's a possibility a third comic will be eventually created, as there is a third world he has yet to have a story take place in, but nothing has been confirmed as of this writing.

An illustration from WLOP's first comic, Ghost Blade. Shows a Princess and her knights.

Animated Wallpapers

WLOP started experimenting with combining 2D and 3D art in order to create animations back in April of 2019. He has since made quite a few animated wallpapers, oftentimes bringing a static artwork of his to life. There are a few pieces that he made exclusively animated, but they are in the minority. Almost all of these pieces of art can be found on his YouTube for free.

This video showcases an animated wallpaper that WLOP created. It combines 2D and 3D animation

Fanart

Although definitely in the minority, WLOP has been known to release fanart of characters from popular franchises. Sometimes this is because the character/franchise in question piques his interest, and sometimes it's commissioned work for one of his many fans. Either way, it's always very cool to see known and often beloved characters brought to life in his unique artstyle.
